Update the Growth Insights conversation starting from the **Financial Insights section**.

The current prototype moves too quickly from financial insights directly to bottlenecks. Improve this section so the financial analysis feels more analytical and gives the SME a moment to reflect.

Replace the current Financial Insights section with a richer card that includes a few simple financial indicators.

Display a card titled:

FINANCIAL INSIGHTS

Include the following insights based on the uploaded financial statements and bank data.

Key financial indicators:

Revenue concentration: 62% of revenue comes from the top 3 buyers
Seasonal cash pressure: cash balances drop during fruit purchasing months
Working capital cycle: approximately 75–90 days between purchasing fruit and receiving payment
Gross margin estimate: approximately 28–32% depending on product mix
Margin improvement potential: premium snack products could improve margins

Below the indicators show a short explanation.

After the card appears Allyra sends the following message:

"Your financial information reveals a few important patterns.

The business appears to have strong demand, but your operating cycle is quite tight. A significant portion of revenue comes from a small group of repeat buyers, which is positive but also creates some dependency.

Cash flow also appears to tighten during fruit purchasing season when you need to buy raw product before it is sold."

Then Allyra asks the SME a reflection question:

"Does this match what you experience during the year?

For example, are there specific months where cash flow becomes more difficult even though demand is strong?"

Allow the user to respond.

"Another interesting pattern is that your margins could likely improve with slightly higher value product variations such as premium snack packs or bundled products for hospitality and tourism markets."

Then Allyra asks:

"Have you experimented with higher value packaging or premium product variations before?"

Allow the user to respond.
