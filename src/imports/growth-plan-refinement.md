Refine the **Build Your Growth Plan** experience so that Allyra constructs the plan collaboratively with the SME rather than presenting a fully completed roadmap.

The growth plan should be built progressively on the canvas while Allyra discusses each step in chat.

---

JOURNEY TRACKER BEHAVIOUR

When the user enters Step 4: **Build Your Growth Plan**, collapse the earlier journey steps.

Instead of showing all steps, display only:

Build Your Growth Plan (active)
Grow Your Business (upcoming)

The earlier steps should appear completed and collapsed so the user focuses on the planning stage.

---

CANVAS INITIAL STATE

When the user arrives on the Growth Plan page, the canvas should be mostly empty.

Display only the page header:

Growth Plan

Subtitle:

Turning insights into practical actions for your business.

No initiatives should appear yet.

---

STEP 1 — DEFINE THE GROWTH FOCUS

Allyra begins by proposing the core growth direction based on the analysis.

Chat message:

"Based on everything we've discussed, I'd like to start by defining the main growth focus for your business."

"From the analysis it appears that demand for your dried fruit products is strong, but growth is currently constrained by processing capacity and seasonal working capital."

"I also see a clear opportunity to expand your retail and tourism distribution."

"Based on this, the core focus I see is:

Expand retail distribution while increasing fruit drying capacity to support growing demand."

At this point the **Growth Focus card appears on the canvas**.

Growth Focus

Expand regional retail and tourism distribution while increasing fruit drying capacity to support growing demand for natural snack products.

Primary constraint
Processing capacity and seasonal working capital.

Secondary opportunity
Retail and tourism distribution growth.

Allyra then asks:

"Does this feel like the right focus for the next phase of your business?"

Wait for the SME response before continuing.

---

STEP 2 — BUILD INITIATIVE 1

After agreement, Allyra introduces the first initiative.

Chat message:

"The first priority is addressing the processing capacity constraint we identified."

The first initiative card then appears on the canvas.

Initiative 01
Increase fruit drying capacity

Description:

Demand for dried fruit snacks is strong, but current drying capacity limits how much fruit can be processed during peak harvest periods.

Actions then appear gradually:

Assess additional fruit drying equipment options
Owner: Operations Manager
Timeline: 30 April 2026

Cost equipment and installation
Owner: Founder + Operations Manager
Timeline: 15 May 2026

Secure equipment financing or lease option
Owner: Founder
Timeline: 15 June 2026

After the actions appear Allyra asks:

"Does this capture the production constraint we discussed?"

"Would you adjust anything here?"
